You feel like a place I don’t have to earn.
Like something familiar I didn’t grow up with,
but somehow always knew.

With you, silence doesn’t rush me.
I don’t have to explain why I’m tired,
or pretend I’m stronger than I am.

You feel like coming back after a long day
and finally taking your shoes off.
Like knowing someone will understand
even when words fall short.

If the world gets loud,
I hope you remember this—
that somewhere, in some quiet corner,
you are home to someone.

And that someone is me.
